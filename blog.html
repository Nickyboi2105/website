Aspirations

Being a Freshman in a Bay Area Highschool has its pros and cons. My aspirations are currently twofold. I have set my eyes on getting into UC Berkley's Computer Science Program immediately after Highschool & not community college. With such passion for problem-solving I aim to become the CEO of a thriving tech company and make major contributions to the field of CS.
